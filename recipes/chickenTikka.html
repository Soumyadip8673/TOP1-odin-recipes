Chicken Tikka

Description

Chicken Tikka is a classic Indian appetizer that features boneless, skinless chicken pieces marinated in a flavorful mixture of yogurt, lemon juice, and Indian spices. The marinated chicken is then grilled in a tandoor (clay oven), giving it a smoky and charred flavor.

The combination of tender chicken, tangy marinade, and smoky flavor makes chicken tikka a popular and satisfying dish. It's often served with dipping sauces like mint chutney or tamarind chutney to enhance the taste.

  • Ingredients

    • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
    • 1 cup plain yogurt
    • 1/4 cup lemon juice
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
    • Salt to taste
    • Skewers

  • Steps

    1. Marinate the chicken: In a large bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, cumin, coriander, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Add the chicken cubes and mix well to coat. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for better flavor.
    2. Thread the chicken: Thread the marinated chicken cubes onto skewers.
    3. Grill the chicken: Preheat your grill or broiler. Grill the skewers for 5-7 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
    4. Serve: Serve the hot chicken tikka with your favorite dipping sauce, such as mint chutney or tamarind chutney.

Enjoy your delicious homemade chicken tikka!
